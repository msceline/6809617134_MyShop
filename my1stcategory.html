Tea


Matcha

Authentic Japanese matcha, finely ground from premium green tea leaves. Whisked to perfection for a bold, earthy flavor and a smooth, umami-rich finish. Served traditionally, hot or iced.


Matcha latte

Smooth and earthy Japanese green tea blended with creamy milk. Rich in antioxidants, naturally energizing, and beautifully balanced—served hot or iced.


Thai tea

A classic Thai favorite brewed from strong black tea, infused with aromatic spices and sweetened just right. Rich, creamy, and delightfully refreshing—served iced for the perfect tropical treat.


Honey lemon tea

A comforting blend of natural honey and fresh lemon, served with warm water or chilled over ice. Lightly sweet, refreshingly tangy, and perfect for a relaxing moment any time of day.


Jasmine tea

Fragrant green tea infused with the delicate aroma of jasmine blossoms. Light, floral, and calming—perfect for a peaceful break, served hot or iced.


Chamomile tea

A naturally caffeine-free herbal tea made from dried chamomile flowers. Lightly floral with a hint of apple-like sweetness, this calming brew is perfect for relaxation—especially in the evening.


Rose tea

A delicate herbal infusion made from dried rose petals. Naturally caffeine-free with a soft floral aroma and gentle sweetness, this elegant tea is perfect for calming the mind and lifting the spirit.


Oolong tea

A semi-oxidized tea with a rich, smooth flavor and subtle floral notes. Balanced between green and black tea, oolong offers a naturally aromatic and refreshing experience—perfect hot or iced.



Coffee


Espresso

A bold and intense shot of pure coffee, expertly extracted to deliver rich aroma, full-bodied flavor, and a velvety crema on top. The perfect base for your favorite coffee drinks or a powerful pick-me-up on its own.


Americano

A smooth and balanced coffee made by diluting a rich espresso shot with hot water. Light enough for easy sipping but still packed with bold coffee flavor—perfect for those who want a longer, mellow coffee experience.


Coffee latte

A smooth and creamy blend of rich espresso and steamed milk, topped with a delicate layer of velvety foam. Perfectly balanced, comforting, and ideal for any time of day.


Mocha

A delicious blend of rich espresso, steamed milk, and smooth chocolate syrup, topped with whipped cream. Perfect for chocolate lovers who want a sweet and indulgent coffee treat.


Cappuccino

A classic Italian coffee drink combining a shot of rich espresso with equal parts steamed milk and airy, frothy foam. Bold, creamy, and perfectly balanced for a satisfying coffee experience.


Macchiato

A bold espresso “stained” with just a touch of steamed milk or foam, delivering an intense coffee flavor with a hint of creaminess. Perfect for those who love a strong yet smooth coffee experience.


Affogato

A delightful Italian treat combining a rich shot of hot espresso poured over creamy vanilla ice cream. The perfect balance of bold coffee and sweet indulgence.


Orange coffee

A unique and refreshing blend of bold espresso infused with bright citrus notes of fresh orange zest. This vibrant combination offers a perfect balance of rich coffee flavor and zesty sweetness—an energizing twist for your coffee break.



Soda


Blue Hawaii soda

A vibrant and refreshing tropical soda bursting with sweet pineapple and tangy citrus flavors, topped with sparkling bubbles and a stunning blue hue. Perfect for cooling down and brightening your day.


Honey lemon soda

A refreshing sparkling drink combining the natural sweetness of honey with the zesty brightness of fresh lemon. Light, bubbly, and perfectly balanced—an uplifting treat to quench your thirst.


Red syrup soda

A classic Thai favorite—sweet red syrup mixed with sparkling soda water, creating a vibrant and refreshing drink with a nostalgic taste. Perfectly fizzy, sweet, and colorful, just like a traditional Thai street drink.


Strawberry soda

A refreshing sparkling drink bursting with natural strawberry flavor and a perfect balance of sweet and tangy. Light, bubbly, and delightfully fruity—an ideal thirst quencher on any day.


Mango soda

A vibrant and refreshing sparkling drink infused with the sweet, tropical flavor of ripe mango. Lightly bubbly and perfectly balanced—an uplifting tropical treat to brighten your day.



Juice


Orange juice

Freshly squeezed from ripe, juicy oranges for a bright and natural burst of citrus flavor. Pure, refreshing, and packed with vitamin C—perfect for a healthy boost any time of day.


Strawberry juice

Freshly blended from ripe, sweet strawberries for a naturally sweet and tangy flavor. Vibrant, refreshing, and packed with antioxidants—perfect for a delicious and healthy treat.


Mix-fruits juice

A refreshing blend of freshly squeezed seasonal fruits, bursting with natural sweetness and vibrant flavors. Packed with vitamins and antioxidants for a healthy boost.


Mango juice

Freshly squeezed from ripe, juicy mangoes for a naturally sweet and tropical flavor. Smooth, rich, and refreshing—an ideal drink to brighten your day.


Kiwi juice

Freshly blended from ripe kiwis, delivering a tangy and slightly sweet flavor with a vibrant green hue. Packed with vitamin C and antioxidants for a refreshing and healthy boost.


Passion fruit juice

Freshly extracted from ripe passion fruits, offering a vibrant, tangy-sweet flavor with tropical floral notes. Refreshing and packed with vitamins—perfect for a revitalizing boost.


Apple juice

Freshly pressed from crisp, sweet apples, delivering a clean and naturally refreshing flavor. Lightly sweet and packed with vitamins—a classic favorite for any time of day.
